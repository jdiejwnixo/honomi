The Story of Our Rice

Honomi is a fully organic Japanese rice grown in Bali with one simple mission: to support local farmers while helping protect Bali’s agricultural land and natural ecosystems. By creating high-value Japanese rice together with local farmers, we aim to make farming more sustainable, profitable, and worth preserving for future generations.

Every grain of Honomi rice is cultivated with deep respect for the Earth, following the natural wisdom of chemical-free agriculture.
